Stay Safe Online

Cyber Safety Tips

Simple habits that can protect you from phishing attacks.

Verify HTTPS

Always ensure websites use HTTPS before entering passwords or payment information.

Inspect the Domain

Fake websites often contain extra words, numbers or misspellings.

Avoid Unknown Links

Never click suspicious links received by email, SMS or social media.

Use Multi-Factor Authentication

Enable MFA whenever possible to protect your online accounts.
